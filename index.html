This frustration led to the creation of RankPredictor, a website that allows you to predict your rank for various exams with no nonsense.

Our Methodology

We gather verified and accurate data from past exam results to ensure our predictions are reliable. Using various mathematical methods, such as linear interpolation, we aim to provide the most accurate rank predictions possible. Detailed explanations of our rank prediction process for each exam are available in the "How does this work?" section within each exam's predictor, complete with graphs and datasets for transparency and scrutiny.
